TrKr Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026
Operated by: TYP Services LLC

1. Introduction

TrKr ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") is a personal finance dashboard that helps you track your portfolio, budget, dividends, and net worth. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the TrKr mobile application. By using TrKr, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide: Account information (name, email, password), phone number (if using SMS 2FA), property addresses (if using home value feature), budget categories and savings goals you create.

Information from Plaid: When you connect a bank or brokerage account, we use Plaid Inc. to securely access your financial data including account names, types, balances, transaction history, and investment holdings. We do NOT receive or store your bank login credentials. Plaid handles all authentication directly. See Plaid's privacy policy.

Information from Zillow (Pro): If you use the home value feature, we send your property address to Zillow to retrieve a Zestimate valuation. See Zillow's privacy policy.

Automatically Collected: Device identifiers (for biometric enrollment), IP address (for security logging), anonymized app usage analytics.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information ONLY to: display your financial dashboard, sync account data via Plaid, calculate net worth and projections, send security notifications (2FA codes), and process your TrKr Pro subscription.

We do NOT: sell your personal information, share data for advertising, use financial data for credit decisions, or share information with employers or insurers.

4. Data Sharing

We share information only with service providers necessary to operate the App: Supabase (database), Plaid (bank linking), Zillow (home values), Apple (app distribution), RevenueCat (subscriptions), SendGrid (email), and Twilio (SMS). We may also disclose information if required by law.

5. Data Security

All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest (AES-256). Database hosted on Supabase (AWS, SOC 2 Type II). Authentication tokens stored in iOS Secure Enclave. Multi-factor authentication required. Row Level Security ensures per-user data isolation.

6. Data Retention

Active account data retained while account is active. Transaction history retained 24 months. Security logs retained 12 months. Deleted account data purged within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to: access your data, correct your information, delete your account and all data, export your data (Pro), disconnect any linked account, and opt-out of data sale (we do not sell data). These rights apply to all users including CA (CCPA/CPRA), VA, CO, CT residents, and EEA (GDPR).

8. Data Deletion

To delete your account: use Settings > Legal > Data Deletion Request, or email privacy@trkr.fyi. All data permanently deleted within 30 days, all Plaid connections revoked, all biometric enrollments cleared. This is irreversible.

9. Children's Privacy

TrKr is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

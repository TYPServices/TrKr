TrKr Data Deletion Policy

Last Updated: April 13, 2026
Operated by: TYP Services LLC

How to Delete Your Data

Option 1 — In-App: Settings > Legal & Privacy > Data Deletion Request

Option 2 — Email: Send a request to privacy@trkr.app with your registered email. We respond within 30 days.

What Gets Deleted

Upon deletion request, we permanently remove: your account profile, all linked bank connections (Plaid access revoked), investment holdings, transaction history, budget categories and goals, debt records, property records, biometric device enrollments, backup codes, and security logs associated with your account.

Timeline

Immediate: Account disabled, sessions terminated.
Within 24 hours: Plaid connections revoked, biometric enrollments cleared.
Within 30 days: All database records permanently deleted. Confirmation email sent.

What We May Retain

We may retain minimal data if required by law, to resolve disputes, or prevent fraud. Only the minimum necessary data is kept and deleted once the obligation ends.

Plaid Data

You can also independently revoke TrKr's access to your bank data via Plaid Portal.

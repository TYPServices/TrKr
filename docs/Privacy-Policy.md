TRKR Private Policy
Last Updated: April 11, 2026
Operated by: TYP Services LLC
1. Introduction
TrKr ("the App," "we," "us," or "our") is a personal finance dashboard that helps you track your portfolio, budget, dividends, and net worth. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the TrKr mobile application.
By using TrKr, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
2. Information We Collect
2.1 Information You Provide
•	Account information: name, email address, password
•	Phone number: if using SMS-based 2FA
•	Property addresses: if using the home value feature
•	Budget categories and amounts you create
•	Savings goals and debt information you enter
2.2 Information from Plaid
When you connect a bank or brokerage account, we use Plaid Inc. ("Plaid") to securely access your financial data. Through Plaid, we receive:
•	Account names, types, and balances
•	Transaction history (date, merchant, amount, category)
•	Investment holdings (ticker, quantity, price, cost basis)
•	Account and routing numbers (for identity verification only)
We do not receive or store your bank login credentials. Plaid handles all authentication directly with your financial institution.
For Plaid's privacy practices, see: https://plaid.com/legal/#end-user-privacy-policy
2.3 Information from Zillow (Pro feature)
If you use the home value feature, we send your property address to Zillow Group via the Bridge Interactive API to retrieve the Zestimate home valuation. See Zillow's privacy policy: https://www.zillowgroup.com/zg-privacy-policy/
2.4 Automatically Collected Information
•	Device identifiers (for biometric enrollment)
•	IP address (for security logging)
•	App usage analytics (anonymized)
3. How We Use Your Information
We use your information only to:
•	Display your financial dashboard (portfolio, budget, dividends, net worth)
•	Sync your account balances and transactions via Plaid
•	Calculate your net worth and financial projections
•	Send security notifications (2FA codes, login alerts)
•	Process your TrKr Pro subscription
•	Improve the application's features and performance
We do not:
•	Sell your personal information to third parties
•	Share your data for advertising or marketing purposes
•	Use your financial data for credit decisions
•	Share your information with employers or insurers
4. Data Sharing
We share your information only with these service providers, and only as necessary to operate the App:
Provider	Purpose	Data Shared
Supabase	Database and authentication	All app data
Plaid	Bank account linking	Account data accessed via Plaid*
Zillow	Home valuation	Property address
Apple	App distribution, in-app purchases	Purchase data
RevenueCat	Subscription management	User ID, subscription status
SendGrid	Email notifications	Email address
Twilio	SMS notifications	Phone number
* Plaid handles authentication directly; we never receive or store your bank login information.
We may also disclose information if required by law, subpoena, or legal process.
5. Data Storage and Security
•	All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+)
•	All data encrypted at rest (AES-256)
•	Database hosted on Supabase (AWS infrastructure, SOC 2 Type II certified)
•	Authentication tokens stored in iOS Secure Enclave
•	Multi-factor authentication required for all users
•	Row Level Security ensures data isolation per user
•	See our Information Security Policy for full details
6. Data Retention
•	Active account data: retained while account is active
•	Transaction history: retained for 24 months from transaction date, then automatically purged
•	Security audit logs: retained for 12 months
•	Plaid access tokens: retained while the bank connection is active; deleted upon disconnection
•	Deleted account data: purged within 30 days of an account deletion request (see Section 8)
7. Your Rights
•	Access: View all data we hold about you (available in Settings > Data Export)
•	Correction: Update your personal information at any time in the App
•	Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data (see Section 8)
•	Portability: Export your data in CSV format (Pro feature)
•	Disconnect: Remove any linked bank account at any time via Settings > Linked Accounts
•	Opt-out of sale: We do not sell your data, but you may submit a "Do Not Sell" request
•	Revoke consent: Withdraw consent for data collection at any time by deleting your account
These rights apply to all users, including residents of:
•	California (CCPA/CPRA)
•	Virginia (VCDPA)
•	Colorado (CPA)
•	Connecticut (CTDPA)
•	European Economic Area (GDPR)
8. Data Deletion
To delete your account and all associated data, use one of the options below:
1.	In-app: Settings > Legal & Privacy > Data Deletion Request
2.	Email: Send a request to privacy@trkr.app and include your registered email address. Response time: within 30 days.
Upon deletion:
•	Your Supabase account and all database records are permanently deleted
•	All Plaid connections are revoked
•	All biometric device enrollments are cleared
•	Backup codes are destroyed
•	This action is irreversible
9. Children's Privacy
TrKr is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 18, we will delete it promptly.
10. Consent
By creating a TrKr account, you consent to:
•	Collection of the data described in Section 2
•	Use of the data as described in Section 3
•	Sharing with service providers as described in Section 4
•	Storage and security practices described in Section 5
You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account (Section 8).
For Plaid specifically: when you connect a bank account, Plaid displays its own consent screen showing exactly what data will be accessed. You must explicitly approve this before any data is shared with TrKr.
11. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes via in-app notification or email. Continued use of TrKr after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
